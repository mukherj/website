Durga in peace
Dimensions : 14 x 18 inches

The Hindu Goddess Durga is a symbol of power and strength who is the root cause of creation, preservation and annihilation. She puts an end to the vicious demon mahisasura (in the form of a buffalo) and regains harmony in all the worlds. This painting portrays her state of peace after victory

Bless The Body And Soul
Dimensions : 16 x 20 inches

This work is inspired from patua art which dates back centuries. It is a form of art from West Bengal, India and it usually narrates a story from art scripts. My work brings in the thought where a young lady still in her dreams bless her little one who is yet to see light ... come to this world and bind its self body and soul together


The four absolutes
Dimensions : 8 x 10 inches

The four moral standards of absolute honesty, purity, unselfishness and love though recognized as impossible to attain, were guidelines to help determine whether a course of action was directed by God. This is an angel from the first dream whose features portray the four absolutes

The Perturbed Mind
Dimensions : 36 x 24 inches

This painting is done in oil portraying the facial expression of a perturbed mind brought up in the form of colors and patterns. Whenever I was overwhelmed by an emotion I sat down to paint. So each section of the painting brings up a different feeling and mood
